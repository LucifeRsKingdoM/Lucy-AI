Open Source AI Education

Lucy AI is more than just a language learning app - it's a movement towards democratizing AI-powered education. Built with passion for open source development and a commitment to making quality education accessible worldwide.

Our mission is simple: break down language barriers and empower people through technology. Every line of code is open source, every feature is free, and every improvement comes from our amazing community.

  • 100% Open Source - View and contribute to our code
  • No Data Collection - Your privacy is our priority
  • Community Driven - Built by learners, for learners
  • Always Free - Education should never have a price tag
